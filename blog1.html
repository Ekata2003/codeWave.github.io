3 Linux Types

Because the Linux operating system is open and released under the GNU General Public License (GPL), anyone can start, read, modify, and redistribute source code, or sell copies of his modified code. This is in stark contrast to the traditional operating systems — Unix, Microsoft Windows, and MacOS — that are more intellectual and less cluttered.

Widely used types of Linux:

  1. Kali linux
  2. Debian or Parrot linux
  3. Ubuntu Os linux

Why Choose Linux only ?

Choosing the right Linux distribution depends on your operating environment and tool requirements. Some Linux distros work better for different purposes. Some distros are designed as desktop areas and some are designed to support background IT applications (such as business or web servers).
When choosing your next Linux distro, your first consideration should be whether you need a Linux distro for business or Linux distro for the community.
